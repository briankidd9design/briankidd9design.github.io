Mission Statement

Online Developer Plus, Inc. a company with three Microsoft Alumni partners, offers online programming courses with different technologies to colleges, individuals and companies. Our Self Paced online courses are designed to provide the necessary information for students to obtain a position in the Hi-Tech industry. We are partnered with several national employment agencies. The U.S. Bureau of Labor projects that up to one million jobs for programming will go unfilled by 2020.

Students can earn a Certificate and become certified if they pass the final online test. They will earn college course credits for each course when they take the course from our college partners. Great move from product support jobs or to earn a promotion to Developer or Program Manager by taking the Software Test Development Engineer program. Also our Program Manager course covers more information about the Cloud. The Python Programming course is perfect for individuals to update their programming skills. If they already know C++, C or C# the Python Programming course will take less time to learn.

Take advantage of our Corporate program with group pricing to upgrade employees’ skills. Great for employment agencies that would like to update workers’ skills.

Strategy

Why Software Engineering Plus?

There are three main reasons why there is currently a shortage of programmers. Software Engineering Plus, SEP has identified these areas and has added them to their business plan.

The first is finding the person that is suited for being a programmer. SEP focuses on where these people go to learn. SEP will partner with the library system to provide a free Intro to C# that students can check out for two months period. We will provide the online instructor as part of the process. At the end of their class we give them an online test to see if they are good candidates for more programming training. Inside each library copy of the course will be an ad for the rest of our online courses.

The second is finding a larger group of individuals as prospects. SEP will partner with Community Colleges. In the past I’ve found that colleges do an excellent job filling their programming classes. SEP will provide ad materials to the colleges to print in their catalogs. This has worked in the past with Mark’s other college partners. Each Software Testing course in the past always sold out and produced some of his best placements for contract jobs at Microsoft.

The last problem to getting good programming candidates is the cost of the education. SEP prices are way lower than all the boot camp’s pricing. The boot camp’s classes use different computer hardware then IBM clones and their programming languages are not the popular compared to Microsoft’s languages.

SEP online courses are developed so the student can study for getting their MCP from Microsoft testing. We plan to hire Microsoft Certified Trainers to develop and do the recordings for our online software. We plan to use Ispring Solutions software for the courses, online testing and also their online registration and credit card processing thru PayPal. 90% of Mark’s students in his last company used credit cards to pay for their courses at InterTest.com. We will also provide a finance company for students who take more than one class or more. The Intro to C# is discounted at $450 so the students can get a good idea that their skills are good enough to take more courses. Boot Camps charge you for the whole price upfront which can be $13K or more.

SEP plans to also place these students and create a special group of talent so that they come back for more education and also network with us for future jobs.
